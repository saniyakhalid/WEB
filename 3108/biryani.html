Delicious Chicken Biryani

A flavorful, aromatic rice dish perfect for gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • Spices: 1 tbsp garam masala, 1 tsp turmeric, 1 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp oil or ghee
  • Fresh cilantro and mint for garnish

Instructions

  1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, spices, and half the onions for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook rice until 70% done and set aside.
  3. In a pot, fry remaining onions until golden, add marinated chicken, and cook until tender.
  4. Layer rice over chicken, add saffron or food coloring for color, and steam for 20 minutes.
  5. Garnish with cilantro and serve hot.

Enjoy your homemade biryani! Serves 4.
