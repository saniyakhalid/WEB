Homemade Vanilla Ice Cream

Creamy, rich vanilla ice cream made from scratch – perfect for a cool treat.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup whole milk
  • 3/4 cup granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
  • Optional: 4 egg yolks for a custard base (for richer texture)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, whisk together the cream, milk, sugar, vanilla, and salt until sugar dissolves.
  2. If using egg yolks, temper them by slowly adding hot cream mixture while whisking, then return to heat and cook until thickened (about 170°F).
  3. Chill the mixture in the fridge for at least 2 hours.
  4. Pour into an ice cream maker and churn according to machine instructions (usually 20-30 minutes).
  5. Transfer to a container, freeze for 4 hours or until firm, and serve.

Enjoy your creamy homemade ice cream! Makes about 1 quart (serves 8).
